Who we are

Full Circle Campus Food Pantry was established by the Volunteer Action Center in February 2011 as a student-run emergency food assistance program that distributes food and personal products to all members of the University of Arkansas community.

Who do we serve

Anyone with a University ID; student, faculty or staff, is welcome to use the pantry. Please remember to bring your ID with you to pick up food.

How to get involved

Full Circle is made possible by student volunteers who commit to serving two hours weekly for a semester. Volunteers greet clients, fill orders, stock donations, etc. Volunteer applications are available at service.uark.edu during the first week of each semester.

Request food

Request forms allow clients to inform us of their food preferences, dietary restrictions and family size. Clients will receive a three day supply of food for their family each time a request is made. Food and personal items available vary. Food requests can be made in person and filled while you wait, or completed online for pick-up at the pantry.
