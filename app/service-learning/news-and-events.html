Committee Working to Formalize Service Learning at U of A

The University of Arkansas is working to increase and organize opportunities for students and faculty to take part in programs that both teach and serve community needs. The U of A Service Learning Initiative is a joint effort by the Office of the Provost, the Honors College and the Division of Student Affairs, and represents a partnership between academic and student affairs.
