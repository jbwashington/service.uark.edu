Volunteers:

Looking for a place to volunteer? Our website is the place to start! Volunteer.uark.edu is a central hub for over 180 non-profit agencies and organizations in Northwest Arkansas to list their service projects. You can search by interests such as animals, kids, environmental, or one-time projects. We hope this makes the search process much easier. Feel free to call or visit us and we can consult with you to find a project that fits your needs. And be sure to log and track your hours after you volunteer!

Agencies:

Are you hoping to engage UA Students as volunteers in your agency or with your program? Thousands of UA students use Volunteer.uark.edu to find their service projects for their Greek Organizations, Registered Student Organizations, and for their own personal ongoing volunteer experience. To get started create your account and post your volunteer needs for students to view. Please be sure to confirm students’ hours after they volunteer with your agency! Chancellor's Community Service Award

The Chancellor's Community Service Award is given to students who spent between 50 and 99 hours during the school year serving their community.  

Minimum Requirements:

  • Recipient must log between 50 and 99 hours through volunteer.uark.edu and have those hours verified by the participating organization.   
  • Recipient(s) must be a student at the University of Arkansas.
  • Awards are issued for service hours served between July 1, 2014 and April 1, 2015.
  • Awards are issued for volunteer service only;  mandated community service does not qualify for the award.

 

 

 

President's Volunteer Service Award

The President's Volunteer Service Award is given to students who spent a minimum of 100 hours during the school year serving their community.  

Minimum Requirements:

  • Recipient must log 100 hours through volunteer.uark.edu and have those hours verified by the participating organization.  
  • Recipient(s) must be a United States citizen or a lawfully admitted permanent resident of the United States and be a student at the University of Arkansas.
  • Awards are issued for service hours served between July 1, 2014 and April 1, 2015.
  • Awards are issued for volunteer service only;  mandated community service does not qualify for the award.
