Volunteer Service Awards

The Chancellor's Community Service Award recognizes University of Arkansas students who have completed 50-99 hours of community service during the school year. Recipients will receive a certificate and letter signed by Chancellor G, David Gearhart recognizing their service to the community.

Minimum Requirements:

  • Recipient must log between 50 and 99 hours throughGivePulseand have those hours verified by the participating organization.
  • Recipient(s) must be a student at the University of Arkansas.
  • Awards are issued for service hours given between July 1, 2014 and April 1, 2015.
  • Awards are issued for volunteer service only; mandatedcommunity service does not qualify for the award.

Applications to obtain this award will be available on or before April 1, 2015. To check how many hours you currently have, log onto your account at GivePulse. Please send any questions to vac@uark.edu or call us at 479-575-4365.

The President's Volunteer Service Award recognizes individuals who have completed 100 or more hours of community service during the school year. Recipients will receive a certificate, a letter signed by President Barack Obama, and a pin recognizing their service to the community.

Minimum Requirements:

  • Recipient must log 100 hours through GivePulse and have those hours verified by the participating organization.
  • Recipient(s) must be a United States citizen or a lawfully admitted permanent resident of the United States and be a student at the University of Arkansas.
  • Awards are issued for service hours given between July 1, 2014 and April 1, 2015.
  • Awards are issued for volunteer service only; mandated community service does not qualify for the award.

Applications to obtain this award will be available on or before April 1, 2015. To check how many hours you currently have, log onto your account at GivePulse. Please send any questions to vac@uark.edu or call us at 479-575-4365.
