Who?

The VAC program is student- program comprised of: Literacy Committee (Chair, Intern, Logistics Coordinator, Curriculum Coordinator, Communications Coordintor, FIVE Volunteer coordinators) Lead mentors, Student volunteers.

What?

Volunteers implement weekly programming with literacy resources and activities and assist their student with reading or homework while building a mentor relationship throughout the semester.

When?

Students volunteer one day a week (Monday- Thursday) from 3:30-4:30pm at a local elementary school for one semester.

Where?

The program is currently active at 5 Fayetteville elementary locations:

  • Leverett Elementary
  • Asbell Elementary
  • Holcomb Elementary
  • Washington Elementary
  • Owl Creek Elementary
