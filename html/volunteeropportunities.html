Volunteers:

Looking for a place to volunteer? Our website is the place to start! Volunteer.uark.edu is a central hub for over 180 non-profit agencies and organizations in Northwest Arkansas to list their service projects. You can search by interests such as animals, kids, environmental, or one-time projects. We hope this makes the search process much easier. Feel free to call or visit us and we can consult with you to find a project that fits your needs. And be sure to log and track your hours after you volunteer!

Agencies:

Are you hoping to engage UA Students as volunteers in your agency or with your program? Thousands of UA students use Volunteer.uark.edu to find their service projects for their Greek Organizations, Registered Student Organizations, and for their own personal ongoing volunteer experience. To get started create your account and post your volunteer needs for students to view. Please be sure to confirm students’ hours after they volunteer with your agency! Please contact our staff if you have questions at 479-575-4365.
